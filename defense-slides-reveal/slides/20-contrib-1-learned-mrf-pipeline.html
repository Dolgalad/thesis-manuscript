Proposed idea: predict factor potentials, infer marginals, then decode with structured information.
This slide contrasts with the factorized baseline. The GNN encoder is still present conceptually, but it is not shown as a separate block. Its output is a set of embeddings on the variable-constraint graph. Variable embeddings are mapped to unary factor parameters, while constraint embeddings are mapped to non-unary factor parameters. The resulting learned MRF is passed to LBP or NMF to compute marginals. During guided decimation, fixing variables gives a conditioned MRF, and inference produces conditional marginals for the remaining variables.
