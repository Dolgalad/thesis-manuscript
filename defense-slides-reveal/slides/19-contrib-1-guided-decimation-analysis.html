This slide focuses on the scientific interpretation of the learned MRF on the Set Cover experiments shown in the previous results slide. The predicted theta values separate feasible and infeasible local assignments. Feasible configurations receive higher learned log-potentials, while infeasible configurations are pushed into a lower-potential regime. This supports the claim that the model has learned structured factor potentials, rather than only independent variable scores.
