Encode variables and constraints. Score variables with a GNN. Use scores to construct or guide solutions.
This slide presents the standard neural-predictor pipeline for ILPs. The ILP is represented as a bipartite variable-constraint graph, optionally with coefficient, objective, bound, and right-hand-side features. A GNN predicts scores on the variables, which are then used either directly by a greedy construction heuristic, by rounding and repair, or as guidance inside a state-of-the-art solver.
