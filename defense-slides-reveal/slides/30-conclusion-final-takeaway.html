01 Constraints shape the output space.

This is the final slide. The thesis message is that learning for BILP solution prediction should move beyond independent variable scores. Constraints couple variables, so predictions should represent dependencies. Markov Random Fields provide a way to encode local structure, and approximate inference turns that structure into beliefs that can guide decoding and solver assistance.
