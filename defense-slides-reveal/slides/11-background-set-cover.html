Given a universe \(U\) and subsets \(S_1,\dots,S_m\), choose as few subsets as possible so that every element is covered.

Example minimum cover: \[ \{S_1,S_4\} \]

\[ \begin{aligned} \min_{x} \quad & \sum_{j=1}^{m} x_j \\ \text{s.t.} \quad & \sum_{j : i \in S_j} x_j \geq 1, && \forall i \in U, \\ & x_j \in \{0,1\}, && \forall j \in \{1,\dots,m\}. \end{aligned} \]

\(x_j = 1\) means subset \(S_j\) is selected.
