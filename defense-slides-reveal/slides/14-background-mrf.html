This slide introduces the formal definition of a Markov random field. The main idea is that the joint distribution over variables factorizes according to the cliques of an undirected graph. The previous ILP energy model is therefore an instance of a broader construction: define local energies, combine them additively, and normalize to obtain a distribution. In the exponential family case, each local energy is parameterized by sufficient statistics and learned parameters.
