How to read it

Baseline Direct denoising

The diffusion model outputs solution scores directly, then EGG decodes them once.

MRF augmentation Diffusion + LBP

Denoising is followed by loopy belief propagation, producing feasibility-aware marginals.

Main effect Feasibility jumps

On both SC-S and SC-M, LBP makes most decoded samples feasible, unlike direct denoising.

The important comparison is not the acronym but the decoding signal. Direct denoising gives scores that are often infeasible: only 7.4 percent feasible on SC-S and zero on SC-M. Adding LBP inference after diffusion changes the denoising output into feasibility-aware marginals, raising feasibility to 88.867 percent on SC-S and 84.615 percent on SC-M. The relative gaps also improve, especially when guided decimation is used. For the defense, I would mention MIS only as a caveat orally, because the main story of this slide is Set Cover.
