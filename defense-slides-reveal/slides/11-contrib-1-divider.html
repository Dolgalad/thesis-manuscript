Learning with approximate MRF inference

Learning constraint-aware factor potentials for binary integer linear programs

This slide marks the transition from the background material to the first main contribution of the thesis. The goal of this part is to show how binary ILP solution prediction can be formulated as approximate inference in a learned Markov Random Field. A GNN predicts local factor parameters, differentiable LBP or NMF computes approximate marginals, and these marginals are used to guide decoding.
