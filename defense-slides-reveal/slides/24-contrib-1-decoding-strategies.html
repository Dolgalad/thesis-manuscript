Decimation schedule: rerun inference every step, every \(k\) fixings, or according to a chosen budget.
This slide only introduces vocabulary. Entropy-guided greedy decoding uses the marginal distribution as a confidence signal and fixes variables greedily. Guided decimation goes further: after some variables have been fixed, the MRF is conditioned on that partial assignment and inference is rerun to estimate conditional marginals for the remaining free variables.
