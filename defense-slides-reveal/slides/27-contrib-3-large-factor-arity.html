Scaling issue: large factor arities

Additional direction: scalable MRF parameterizations for high-arity constraints.

Problem: a constraint over d binary variables induces 2d parameters.
Idea: replace large factors by a compact approximate factor graph.
Tradeoff: less expressive, but scalable.
Keep this short. This is not presented as a full third contribution with results. It is an additional direction motivated by the exponential cost of high-arity factor tables, especially for constraints such as Set Cover.
