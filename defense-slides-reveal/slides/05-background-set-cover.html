Given a universe \(U = \{1,\dots,m\}\) and subsets \(S_1,\dots,S_n\), choose as few subsets as possible so that every element is covered.

Example minimum cover: \[ \{S_1,S_4\} \]

\[ \begin{aligned} \min_{x} \quad & \sum_{i=1}^{n} x_i \\ \text{s.t.} \quad & \sum_{i : j \in S_i} x_i \geq 1, && \forall j \in U, \\ & x_i \in \{0,1\}, && \forall i \in \{1,\dots,n\}. \end{aligned} \]

\(x_i = 1\) means subset \(S_i\) is selected.
