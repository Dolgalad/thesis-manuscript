Guided decimation alternates between fixing variables, conditioning the MRF, and recomputing beliefs.
The first fragment shows the initial state: no variable has been fixed and all variables are candidates, so the scores are the current marginals. The graph is already visible and uncolored. The second fragment commits to the selected variable x2, fixes it to one, removes its neighbors to preserve the independent-set constraint, and updates the candidate list. At the same time, the graph state changes without a separate transition. The third fragment shows the next iteration: inference is rerun under the partial solution S, and the scores are conditional marginals for the remaining candidate variables.
