Bottom line MRFs improve the denoising signal, but they do not replace problem-specific decoding.
Keep this slide short. The point is that MRF inference is useful in diffusion-style denoising because the corrupted state is partial information about the target. On Set Cover, LBP-based denoising gives the clearest improvement in feasibility and decoded objective value. The limitation is that refinement alone does not guarantee feasible solutions: the final quality still depends on the decoder and on the problem structure.
